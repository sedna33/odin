Mac and cheese

description

I'ts an american dish with lots of cheese. you can find it in every single store or fast food. its a simple prep meal.

I like to make it for picnic day or when my friends come to hang out in my place

Ingridients

  • 2 cups elbow pasta
  • 1 cup montrey cheese
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese
  • 2 Tbs flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • some oil,pepper and salt

Steps

  1. boil pasta and drain
  2. heat up milk, add flour and wisking quikly until its smooth and thick
  3. add cheese to milk sauce and stir well
  4. add cheesy sauce to cooked pasta
  5. top with rest of the cheese
  6. put it in the oven until top layer of cheese get melted and bubbly
