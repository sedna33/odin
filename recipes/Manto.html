Manto

Description

I am going to write down about my favourite afghan dish that brings me joy and the whole sweet memory of my friends, family, party and my dear Kabul. I always feel such a strong bonding with the whole process of making Manto that all is about artistic details and skilfull performance with dough, spend time joyfully which is my passion for cooking.

Manto is the most popular afghan dish in entire counyry the people used to serve it in wedding, birthday party, gatherig or as a simple daily meal for family or used to order it in restaurant so you can imagine how important it is.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb chopped onion
  • all purpose flouver as you need
  • 1 pinch salt for dough
  • a bounch of cilantro
  • 1 Tbs cilantro seed
  • 1 tbs black pepper
  • 1 lb split peas
  • 1 Tbs tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt

Steps

  1. make your dough and put it aside to rest for 30 minutes
  2. make your sauce first put oil in pan add onion untile get tender add tomato paste and spices: pepper, salt and some fresh cilantro then pour some water and peas and leave it until it is cooked
  3. get another pan put some oil add onion chase it with adding the beef and let it get brown not pink, finally add spices
  4. get back to dough spread it fully thin cut it to squer pieces pick one spoon beef and onion mixture that earlier made it and wrap it. I am going to add few pictures here for showing you how to wrap beef
  5. put them in a steamer pot like this one or whatever you have
  6. let them to be cooked for 20 minutes
  7. when cooking is done put them in a plate, put sauce on it and add a layer of yogurt on top of it and add some cilantro as a garnish and voila
