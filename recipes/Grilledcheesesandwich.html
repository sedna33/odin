Grilled cheese sandwich

Description

Its the most comfort meal or I can say snack I've ever made. i used to make it when I'm watching movie or have no time and need somthing fast and easy

Grilled cheese sandwich with tomato soup is a winner combination and works all the time for me

Ingridients

  • 2 slices wheat or white bread
  • 2 slices american cheese or whatever you like
  • some butter

Steps

  1. heat up a pan and put butter
  2. make cheese and bread like sandwich, put it in the hot pan
  3. let it get golden and crispy
  4. congrats you're done that was pretty easy
